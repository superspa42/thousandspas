WHAT IS ACCOUNTANTCONNECT?
AccountantConnect is a modern community of accountants
In this community, the business owners can easily find the right accountant & right accounting advice for their business.

Also, this will benefit the accountants hugely, by giving them access to more clients.


Here is how it will work for the accountants
      
Step 1. Create your profile
Start by creating your profile on AccountantConnect platform.
You can highlight your area of expertize, upload your photograph, share your customer testimonials and a lot more.
Step 2. We will help businesses find you.
Business owners reach our site when they are in need of Accounant or accounting advice. They can find you directly by searching you on our portal. You can also share articles and help the businesses with their queries. This will get you better visibility.
Step 3. Post informative articles & answer queries.
Post useful articles around accounting and help businesses with their accounting related queries. This will give you better visibility among the businesses on our platform.
That can help you grow your practice!
Our vision is to help accountants grow their practice while helping businesses with their critical accounting needs.
